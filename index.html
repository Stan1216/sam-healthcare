Her platelets are 90,000 and she's had fever 3 days. Should I worry?
I've read the report. A platelet count of 90,000/µL is below the normal range (150,000–450,000) — with 3 days of fever in Hyderabad's monsoon season, this pattern is consistent with a possible viral fever, dengue included, and warrants close monitoring rather than panic. Recommended next step → a home physician review today, and a repeat platelet count in 24 hours to see the trend.
Why I say this · WHO Dengue Guidelines 2009, warning-signs criteria · NVBDCP India Dengue Management 2024 · ICMR platelet-monitoring advisory. View sources
Which specialist if it gets worse?
If platelets fall below 50,000, bleeding appears, or she becomes drowsy — this needs a General Physician / Internal Medicine specialist today, with hospital admission on standby. If it's an emergency, call 108 now. I'll have SAM's care team call you within the hour to arrange the right review. I can't diagnose or prescribe — a qualified clinician confirms every step.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked

What exactly is the 60-minute promise?
Within 60 minutes of your call or booking, a qualified clinician — not a bot, not a call-centre script — has heard your situation, assessed it and set your care plan moving, within our active Hyderabad zones during care hours (8am–10pm). Physiotherapy visits are typically at your door the same day. We grow one zone at a time — never faster than our quality can follow.
Which services are live today?
Physiotherapy & rehabilitation (including pelvic-floor and women's rehab) is serving homes across Hyderabad now. Doctor visits, nursing and diagnostics are opening next — founding clinicians are being credentialed and trained as you read this. Join a priority list and we'll call you the day your service goes live; until then, our care desk will gladly point you to the right option.
Who are your doctors?
MBBS-verified physicians, many with experience at Hyderabad's leading hospitals. Before the visit you receive your doctor's name, photo, qualification and medical-council registration number on WhatsApp.
What does a home visit cost?
₹1,299 flat for a day visit, ₹1,599 at night — published right on this page. Medicines and consumables are billed at MRP and shown to you before they're used. No surprise billing, ever.
What is a Family Care Manager?
One named person who knows your family's full medical story — coordinating every doctor, nurse, physio and lab visit, and keeping family members (including those abroad) updated on WhatsApp.
Do you handle long-term and elder care?
Yes — 8/12/24-hour skilled nursing and attendants, with background-verified staff and a guaranteed replacement within 24 hours if you're not satisfied, plus monthly Elder Care memberships designed for families and NRI children.
What are your care hours?
Doctors and nurses dispatch every day, 8am–10pm, across all active zones. Our 24×7 night-response network is being switched on zone by zone — Elder Care members get first access as each zone goes live. For life-threatening emergencies, always dial 108 first; we coordinate hospital admission alongside.
